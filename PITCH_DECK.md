# NexForensics Pitch Deck Summary

## 1. The Problem
- **Fragile Integrity**: Crime scenes are contaminated easily. Once released, they are gone forever.
- **Juror Confusion**: 2D photos fail to convey spatial relationships (trajectories, lines of sight).
- **Slow Analysis**: Manual cataloging of evidence takes days.

## 2. The Solution: NexForensics
An AI-powered platform that freezes crime scenes in time using 3D reconstruction and automates evidence discovery.

## 3. Key Value Propositions
- **Speed**: 10x faster evidence logging via AI.
- **Accuracy**: Sub-millimeter precision in 3D reconstruction.
- **Admissibility**: Built-in chain of custody logging ensuring court acceptance.

## 4. Target Markets
1. **Law Enforcement Agencies**: Primary SaaS subscribers.
2. **Legal Defense/Prosecution**: "Presentation Mode" licenses.
3. **Forensic Universities**: Educational simulation licenses.

## 5. Revenue Model
- **Agency Tier**: $2,500/mo (Unlimited Cases, 5 Investigators)
- **Enterprise Tier**: Custom (State-wide deployment)
- **Academic Tier**: $50/student/sem

## 6. Vision
To become the global standard for digital forensic evidence presentation by 2030.
